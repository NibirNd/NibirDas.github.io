My Bio

Why did the economist cross the road? To get to the other side of the supply and demand curve! As an economist and mathematics major, I'm always up for a good laugh, but my passion for knowledge and analytical thinking is no joke. Graduating with a BA degree from the University of Alberta, I have spent years diving into the intricacies of our world, using data-driven analysis to make sense of complex economic systems. I am fueled by an insatiable hunger for knowledge and a deep fascination for the complexities of the human mind.

I am excited to continue on this journey of exploration and discovery, and I invite you to join me in the pursuit of knowledge and understanding.
